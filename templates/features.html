Virtual Study Rooms

This website helps students study together online in focused virtual rooms. You can join your friends, share study materials, and stay productive using smart study tools.

How It Works

  1. Create an account using Sign Up.
  2. Login to your dashboard.
  3. Create a study room or join using a room ID.
  4. Choose room options like chat, file sharing, voice, and Pomodoro timer.
  5. Share the room ID with friends.
  6. Study together in real time.

Key Features

Study Rooms

Create private rooms and invite others using a unique room code.

Smart Chat Modes

Choose Quick Chat, Free Chat, or No Chat based on your focus needs.

File Sharing

Upload and download notes, PDFs, and study materials inside the room.

Pomodoro Timer

Use focus and break sessions together to improve productivity.

Member Status

See who is online and actively studying in your room.

Secure Login

Your account is protected using encrypted passwords.

Who Is This For?

This platform is useful for students, friends, online study groups, and anyone who wants to stay focused while studying remotely.

Why Use This Platform?

  • All study tools in one place
  • Encourages focus and teamwork
